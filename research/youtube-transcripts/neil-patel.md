YouTube Video Summary (AI-Powered SEO)
Title idea: “How to Use AI for SEO Without Losing Rankings”

Neil outlines a practical framework for AI-assisted SEO:

Use AI for research acceleration (keywords, intent mapping, competitor gaps)
Keep humans in the loop for strategy, differentiation, and brand voice
Prioritize E-E-A-T signals through expert input and unique examples
Measure impact with rankings + conversion metrics, not traffic alone
Continuously refresh existing pages with AI-supported update workflows
Core message: AI should amplify proven SEO fundamentals, not replace them.
