LinkedIn Post 1 (AI SEO Trend)
One of the biggest misconceptions in AI SEO: publishing more content will automatically drive growth.

What I’m seeing instead:

Sites with strong editorial standards are gaining
Sites using AI at scale without clear value are plateauing or declining
Trust signals, source quality, and author credibility matter more than ever
AI can improve efficiency, but it cannot manufacture authority.
In 2026, quality control is an SEO strategy - not just an editorial preference.

LinkedIn Post 2 (AI SEO Trend)
If you’re adapting to AI-influenced search, start with risk management.

Three priorities I recommend:

Audit AI-generated pages for factual accuracy and citation quality
Strengthen author transparency and expert review policies
Align content to clear user outcomes, not just query matching
Search systems are increasingly better at evaluating usefulness.
The future belongs to brands that combine AI speed with accountable publishing practices.
